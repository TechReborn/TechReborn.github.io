Tech Reborn V is Coming Soon!

Note: The 'V' is not a roman numeral 5. It is in fact the letter 'V', standing for Vanilla Edition.

Unlike classical versions of Tech Reborn, in TRV everything has been optimized to be a light load on anyone's computer. In fact, the clientside requirements to run TR now are exactly the same as for vanilla.

TR now operates SERVERSIDE-ONLY to take advantage of the fact that most Minecraft players play on vanilla.

Since TR is no longer on the clientside, we can no longer add textures for our blocks. To combat this problem, signs will generate around ores to indicate what they actually are. Above, you see some copper ore generating in a cave.

Above is a Generator, which will generate energy using normal furnace fuels.

Using a cable, you can transfer energy between machines as you'd expect.

The energy from the Generator travels to the Grinder, which can grind up ores into dusts. These dusts are then smelted into ingots as you'd expect. Hovering over the redstone dust in the fuel slot will give you stats on the energy the machine is holding.

All of us at Team Reborn are excited to finally share this news with you. You can expect a beta build in the coming weeks. We had planned to release this later but due to COVID-19, we suddenly had a lot of time to work on this project.
